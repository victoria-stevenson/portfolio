About Me



Hi, my name is Vicky! After growing up in Germany, I spent 10 years living in the US, and the last 4 years in Spain. I'm bilingual in English and German, intermediate/conversational in Spanish. Early on, my career was mostly in translation, transcription and content services.

Over the years, my professional background developed into the area of internet marketing, wordpress website development, SEO, autoresponder and email list management, etc.

Since raising my three children for the last few years, I decided to combine my passion for languages and tech into a career in coding. In the spring of 2023 I started the frontend development bootcamp with SheCodesMax, which I have since nearly completed. Next, my plan is to work my way towards the Front-End Developer Professional Certificate by Meta (Facebook).

I absolutely love coding and am excited to start a rewarding career as a developer, to continue learning and improving and to bring my drive and enthusiasm into your team!
